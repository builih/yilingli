Yiling Li

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at Princeton University majoring in Electrical & Computer Engineering with minors in Statistics & Machine Learning, Robotics, and Visual Arts.

My research focuses on advancing light-driven technologies at the intersection of nanophotonics and computational imaging.

I am currently working with Professor Felix Heide at Princeton Computational Imaging Lab for my senior thesis.

Beyond research, I am also the President of Princeton Engineering Council, StudioLab makerspace technician, and previous Artistic Director of Triple 8 Dance Company.
