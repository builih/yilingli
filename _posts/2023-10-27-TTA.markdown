We optimize the dielectric and acceptor layers of organic light-emitting diodes (OLEDs) to make triplet-triplet annihilation photon upconversion (TTA-UC) more efficient. Our final structure was a non-doped annihilator layer sandwiched between two sensitizer layers with no dielectric layer.
